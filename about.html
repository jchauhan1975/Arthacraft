About

I have spent two and a half decades solving the hardest data and technology problems for Fortune 500 and mid-tier companies — the kind of work that now sits at the heart of every serious AI initiative.

In that time I have seen digital transformation done right. And I have seen it become expensive theater — the carefully choreographed slide decks, the vendor-led roadmaps, the initiatives that consume millions and change nothing.

The difference is almost never the technology. It is the strategic rigor — or lack of it — behind every decision.

That is the gap I built my practice around.
